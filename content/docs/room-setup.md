## Troubleshooting

### Speaker Hum

Symptom:
Hum or buzz over the room speakers when using the 3.5mm audio cable.

Resolution:
On the D1 box, flip the "lift" switch. If that fails to resove the issue, replace the D1 box.

Context:
The hum is most likely due to a ground issue.
